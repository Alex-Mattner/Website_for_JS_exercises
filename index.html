Task

How does it work?

With these tasks and challenges you will start your carrer path in WebDev.

Designed for developers

Start small and grow... Even the best developer once started off by declaring variables. So don't worry. Just enjoy the ride.

Take your time

Rome has not been built in one day, so don't rush. Take your time. Sometimes your progress will go faster, sometimes it will go slower. Remember: Growing is not a steady line upwards. Sometimes you just have to practice a topic over and over until you get breakthrough.

Enjoy the journey

If you love what you do you will never have to work again! ...WebDev can be great fun, but only if you let it to be that way. Don't put yourself under preassure. If things don't work...try again. Enjoy it. But never get afraid. If you manage this, you will have a great future in WebDev!

Remember

  • Take it easy
  • Enjoy
  • Don't rush
  • But keep up the work
  • Grow 1% every day
  • Everybody can learn JavaScript
  • All you need to do is ... LOVE IT!!!

  • Alle Eingabefelder, die mit der Klasse 'mandatory' belegt sind, müssen Inhalt haben.
